Painting the Town:
A Touch of Hollywood Glamour

When the historic Playhouse Theater in St. Petersburg needed a visual facelift, the Vitale Bros. brought the golden age of cinema roaring right back to life onto its exterior walls.

Featured in a special segment by Fox 13 Tampa Bay, the spotlight focused on our massive, spray-painted portrait of the iconic actress Marilyn Monroe. "It sort of goes back to the time when this was a hot spot and all this crew, with the rat pack and everything, they all hung out together," Johnny Vitale recounted to the news team.

The segment also highlighted our expansion across the bay, noting our commercial work at spots like the colorful Nueva Cantina in Tampa. Fox 13 beautifully captured what drives our family-run operation. As Paul Vitale noted on-camera, "People always say, 'How can you work with your family?' But I couldn't work with anybody else... we kinda check each other, crack on each other's art, and when all is said and done, it's a better quality piece of art."
