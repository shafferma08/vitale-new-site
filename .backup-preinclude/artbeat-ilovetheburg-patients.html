Power to the Patients:
Shepard Fairey Collaboration

In the spring of 2021, the Vitale Bros joined forces with globally renowned contemporary street artist Shepard Fairey to participate in the impactful national campaign: "Power to the Patients".

This powerful initiative was created to combat the hidden prices of healthcare and advocate for absolute transparency in hospital billing. To help amplify this critical message in St. Petersburg, we painted a striking, large-scale piece located behind the Morean Arts Center at 719 Central Avenue.

The mural features Shepard Fairey's distinct, commanding design language: a defiant raised fist within a bold red circle, crowned with the urgent declaration: "Prices are a Patient’s Right." It was an honor to lend our cans to such a vital, nationwide cause and bring Fairey's signature aesthetic to our hometown blocks.
