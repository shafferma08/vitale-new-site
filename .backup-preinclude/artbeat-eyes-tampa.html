Parking Garages Transformed:
Downtown Tampa

Parking structures are historically gray, utilitarian blocks in urban landscapes. But what happens when you treat massive concrete parking facilities as multi-story canvases?

The prominent street art blog, *Eyes on the Streets*, recently took a photo tour highlighting the visual evolution of Downtown Tampa's parking garages. The spotlight featured the iconic William F. Poe Parking Garage on North Ashley Drive, which boldly houses the "Stay Curious" sequence.

This massive, critically acclaimed installation was created by powerhouse local artists Tes One and Bask, with the Vitale Brothers proud to have contributed our paint and heavy-lifting execution to bring the striking mural to its final form. It's proof that there are no "boring" walls—only large-scale opportunities to inject life into the concrete jungle.
