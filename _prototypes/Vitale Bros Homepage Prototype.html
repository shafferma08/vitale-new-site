How we work

From napkin sketch to finished wall.

Simple, collaborative, and built to keep you confident all the way through. Most projects run four to six weeks start to finish.

01
Brief & Visit
We meet at your space, walk the wall, talk about goals, audience, and budget. Free consultation, no pressure.
02
Sketch & Quote
You get concept sketches and a clear price. We refine together until the direction feels unmistakably yours.
03
Paint
We prep the surface and bring the art to life. Most walls take 3 to 14 days depending on scale. We keep you posted with progress shots.
04
Unveil & Protect
A UV-rated clearcoat keeps the colors alive for 7+ years in Florida sun. We throw in a touch-up guarantee.

It depends on size, detail, and location, but most projects fall between $4,000 for a small interior wall and $40,000+ for stadium or multi-story exterior work. We give you a clear, fixed quote after the first visit. No surprises.
Small indoor pieces are often done in 2 to 4 days. Typical exterior walls run 1 to 3 weeks. Large commercial projects can take a month or more, and we always share a schedule before starting.
With proper surface prep and a UV-rated clearcoat, our exterior murals hold strong color for 7 to 10+ years even in Florida sun and salt air. We include a touch-up guarantee for the first year.
Interior murals generally don't need permits. Exterior murals depend on your city and zoning. We've worked with St. Pete, Tampa, and surrounding municipalities for decades and can help navigate the process.
