What we do

Your home, turned into art.

Backyard escapes, poolside tropicals, pin-up garages, kid's rooms, stairwells. Our residential work ranges from a small indoor accent wall to a full perimeter fence wrapped in a Florida jungle. We've spent 30+ years figuring out what holds up to Tampa Bay rain, Florida sun, splash-out, and sea air.

Most residential projects run two to ten days on site. We bring our own equipment, tarp the area clean, and leave the space protected with a UV-rated clearcoat so the colors stay loud for years.

Pools, fences, and patios Tropical murals built for splash, sun, and humidity.
Interior feature walls Living rooms, stairwells, nurseries, home bars, and garages.
Custom and personal From family portraits to favorite places to whole imagined worlds.
