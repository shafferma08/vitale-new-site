Commercial Mural

Tampa Bay Rays
City Connect

Tropicana Field, St. Petersburg
Sports & Entertainment
Scroll

A massive, heavily detailed celebration of the Tampa Bay Rays' City Connect uniforms. The mural highlights the gritty, authentic skateboarding and street culture of St. Pete, bridging the vibrant energy of the community with the raw excitement of baseball. Featuring intricate character portraits, classic Rays logos, and a kinetic "overspray" style that demands attention.

Client Tampa Bay Rays / MLB
Location Tropicana Field, St. Petersburg, FL
Category Stadium Mural — Sports & Entertainment
