"Murder It" at Blue Goose:
A Family Showcase

Artistic talent doesn't just run in the Vitale family; it practically overflows onto the canvas. The Tampa Bay Times featured our special local exhibition, affectionately titled "Murder It," hosted at the beloved Blue Goose in downtown St. Petersburg.

This dynamic local showcase featured striking, original new works from Johnny, Paul, and Joey Vitale. It was an opportunity for our entire team of brothers to let loose and present fresh, independent studio pieces to the community in an electric, local environment.

Whether creating large-scale street art under the baking Florida sun or smaller, intricate canvases to hang inside St. Pete’s finest independent businesses, our philosophy remains the same: push the envelope, collaborate, and always "Murder It."
